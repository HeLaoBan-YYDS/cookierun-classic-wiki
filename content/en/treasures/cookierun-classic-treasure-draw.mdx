## What is the CookieRun Classic Treasure Draw?

Treasures are equipable items that grant passive or active bonuses during runs. Unlike cookies or pets that have fixed abilities, treasures allow you to customize your gameplay. The **CookieRun Classic treasure draw** is the primary method of acquiring these game-changing items. Players spend in-game currency—either crystals or coins—to pull random treasures from a gacha pool.

In the early stages of the game, players often wonder whether they should invest their hard-earned crystals into cookies, pets, or treasures. According to community reports and veteran player experiences, securing a solid foundation of utility treasures early on is far more beneficial than maxing out a single cookie. This is because treasures can be shared across all cookies in your roster, making them an incredibly versatile investment.

The draw system features different tiers of chests. The cheapest draws cost a small amount of coins or crystals and yield common or rare treasures. The premium draws, which require a significant amount of crystals, guarantee high-tier epic treasures that provide the most powerful score boosts and utility effects in the game. To optimize your **CookieRun Classic treasure draw** strategy, you must know when to buy the cheapest options and when to save up for the premium banners.

## Top-Tier Treasures to Target

When performing a **CookieRun Classic treasure draw**, your goal should be to pull items that directly impact your score output or survival capabilities. Based on gameplay tests from ex-pro players, certain treasures stand out as absolute must-haves.

### Magnetic Aura Treasures
These are the undisputed kings of utility. Magnetic treasures pull in jellies and potions from a distance, preventing you from missing crucial items while dodging obstacles. Equipping double magnetic aura treasures is a popular strategy for high-score runs, as it allows you to focus entirely on navigation without worrying about missing points.

### Potion Generators
These treasures periodically spawn small health potions, extending your run time and allowing you to reach late-stage maps where points are abundant. When paired with high-energy cookies, potion generators can easily add an extra 30 to 60 seconds to your run.

### Score Boosters
These items multiply the points you receive from specific jelly types, such as Bear Jellies, Gold Coins, or Rainbow Jellies. While they do not offer survival utility, they are essential for reaching the top spots on the leaderboard.

### Revival Items
Giving your cookie a second chance after running out of energy is invaluable, especially when trying to squeeze out the last few million points before a run ends. Some revival treasures also grant a temporary shield or speed boost upon activation.

By focusing your **CookieRun Classic treasure draw** pulls on these specific categories, you will build a versatile collection that fits any cookie combination.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How often should I perform a CookieRun Classic treasure draw?
You should perform draws whenever you have a surplus of crystals, but it is highly recommended to save your resources for special rate-up events. During these events, the odds of pulling Epic treasures are significantly higher, giving you more value for your crystals.

### Is the CookieRun Classic treasure draw completely random?
Yes, the draw system operates on a random number generator (RNG). However, premium draws feature a pity system that guarantees at least one Epic treasure after a set number of pulls, ensuring that players eventually get high-tier items.

### What are the best treasures for beginners to target?
Beginners should focus on acquiring magnetic aura treasures and revival items. These treasures provide the most utility, helping you collect jellies automatically and survive longer on difficult maps.

### Can I upgrade treasures without spending real money?
Absolutely. You can earn enhancement stones, tickets, and coins by completing daily missions, participating in events, and running in standard game modes. Upgrading your treasures takes time, but it is entirely possible to max them out as a free-to-play player.
