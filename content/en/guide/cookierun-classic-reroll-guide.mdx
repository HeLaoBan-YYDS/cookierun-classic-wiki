## Account Binding and Saving Your Progress

Once you have successfully pulled your dream team, you must secure your account to prevent losing your progress. Guest accounts are temporary and can be lost if your device updates, crashes, or if you clear your app cache.

This **CookieRun Classic reroll guide** recommends linking your account to DevPlay, Google, Apple, or Facebook. Linking to a DevPlay account is often the best choice because it allows you to easily cross-play on different devices and claim exclusive rewards from other Devsisters titles.

| Account Type | Security Level | Cross-Platform Compatibility | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **DevPlay Account** | Very High | Excellent (iOS, Android, PC) | All players |
| **Google Play / Game Center** | High | Limited to same OS | Mobile-only players |
| **Facebook** | Medium | Good | Social sharing and backup |

To bind your account, tap the three lines in the top-right corner of the main screen to open the menu. Go to **Settings**, select the **Info** tab, and choose your preferred login method under the "Account" section. Follow the prompts to link your email. Once linked, your progress is permanently saved to the cloud. If you ever run into account issues, you can visit the [official Devsisters support portal](https://cs.devsisters.com/) to submit a ticket and recover your data.

To make the most out of your freshly rerolled account, you should manage your resources wisely. The choices you make in the first few hours of gameplay will dictate how quickly you can level up your new team.

| Resource | Primary Source | Best Usage | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Crystals** | Story Quests, Arena, Mailbox | 10x Pulls on Featured Banners | High |
| **Cookie Cutters** | Events, Shop Purchases | Single Pulls on Standard Banner | Medium |
| **Mileage Points** | Gacha Duplicates | Buying Soulstones for missing Epics | High |
| **Star Jellies** | Cookie Houses, Tower of Chaos | Leveling up your core team of 5 | Critical |

### Focus on a Core Team of Five

It is tempting to spread your resources across all the cool Cookies you pull, but this is a common trap. XP Star Jellies become scarce very quickly in the mid-game. Focus all of your leveling resources on your core team of five units. 

### Spend Mileage Points Wisely

Every time you pull duplicates in the Gacha, you earn Mileage Points. Do not waste these points on treasure tickets early on. Instead, use them to purchase **Soulstones** for essential Epic Cookies that you missed during your reroll process. This allows you to slowly piece together a perfect team composition without relying purely on luck.
