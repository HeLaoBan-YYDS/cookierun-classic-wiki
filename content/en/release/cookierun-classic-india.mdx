## Core Gameplay Mechanics and Controls

At its heart, the game is an endless runner that requires precision, timing, and strategy. While the controls are easy to learn—consisting primarily of jumping and sliding—the depth of the game lies in mastering the level layouts and utilizing specific Cookie abilities.

### Navigating the Levels
As you dash through various stages, you will encounter obstacles like pits, spikes, and moving barriers. To survive, you must:
*   **Jump:** Tap the left side of the screen to hop over low-lying obstacles.
*   **Double Jump:** Tap twice in quick succession to reach higher platforms or clear wider gaps.
*   **Slide:** Hold the right side of the screen to duck under hanging obstacles or narrow passages.

Collecting "Jellies" is the primary way to increase your score. There are various types of Jellies, including basic ones, Bear Jellies, and special power-up items that can turn your Cookie into a giant or allow them to fly through the stage.

## Mastering the Competitive Landscape

While the game is accessible to casual players, the competitive "Trophy Race" mode is where the real challenge lies. In this mode, you are matched against players from across the subcontinent to see who can achieve the highest score on a specific track. 

Success in competitive play requires more than just fast reflexes. You must manage your "Energy" bar effectively. Every second you run, your energy depletes. Hitting an obstacle causes a significant chunk of energy to disappear instantly. Therefore, the goal is to find the optimal path that maximizes Jelly collection while minimizing damage.

### Strategies for High Scores
1.  **Level Up Your Cookies:** Use the coins and "Spirit Potions" you earn to increase your Cookie’s level. This increases their base energy and enhances their special skills.
2.  **Upgrade Your Pets:** A high-level Pet activates its skill more frequently, which is crucial for maintaining momentum.
3.  **Treasures:** These are equipable items that provide global buffs, such as extra points for certain Jellies or a "save" if you fall into a pit.
4.  **Learn the Map:** Most levels have "hidden" paths that contain more Jellies or easier obstacles. Memorizing these can give you a competitive edge.

| Shield Bubble | Protects from one hit | Beginners and clumsy runners |
| Turbo Engine | Increases base speed | Speed-focused builds |
| Coin Fireworks | Generates coins during the run | Farming resources |
| Jelly Compass | Points towards secret paths | Exploratory play |

## Progression and Resource Management

As you progress, you will collect various currencies. Managing these wisely is the key to long-term success without feeling the need to spend real money.

*   **Coins:** Used for basic upgrades of Cookies and Pets.
*   **Crystals:** The premium currency used to "Gacha" (pull) for new Epic-tier Cookies and Treasures.
*   **Spirit Potions:** Used to level up specific Cookies when you don't have enough duplicate copies.
*   **Mileage:** Earned by performing Gacha pulls; can be traded for specific high-tier items.

### Daily Checklist for Maximum Growth
*   **Log in Daily:** Claim your daily attendance rewards.
*   **Complete Daily Quests:** These provide a steady stream of Crystals and Coins.
*   **Participate in Breakout Mode:** This mode requires you to use a relay of several Cookies. The further the total distance, the better the weekly reward.
*   **Check the Shop:** Sometimes there are free daily packages or items available for coins that are highly valuable.
