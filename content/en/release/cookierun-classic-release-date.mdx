## Core Gameplay Mechanics and Features

At its heart, the game relies on a simple two-button control scheme: one button to jump (and double-jump) and another to slide. Despite this simplicity, the level design requires precise timing and quick reflexes. As you run, your Cookie's health bar continuously depletes, requiring you to collect Potions scattered throughout the map to keep going.

The primary objective is to collect Jellies, dodge obstacles, and escape the Witch's Oven. Along the way, players can customize their runs using a variety of Cookies, Pets, and Treasures. Pets provide active bonuses during runs, such as generating health potions or destroying obstacles, while Treasures offer passive stat boosts that can significantly increase your high score potential.

Here is a breakdown of the core gameplay loop steps:

| Step | Action | Objective |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Choose Your Setup | Select a Cookie, pair them with a Pet, and equip up to three Treasures. |
| **2** | Run and Dodge | Use Jump and Slide to avoid pits, spikes, and falling obstacles. |
| **3** | Collect Resources | Gather Jellies for points and Health Potions to extend your run time. |
| **4** | Activate Skills | Trigger unique Cookie and Pet abilities to maximize point collection. |
| **5** | Compete in Leagues | Submit your score to the real-time global leaderboard to earn rewards. |

## Tips and Strategies for Rookie Runners

If you are jumping into the game following the **CookieRun Classic release date**, you might want a few tips to help you climb the league leaderboards. The classic formula relies heavily on muscle memory and level memorization, but a few strategic choices can give you an edge.

First, make sure to take advantage of the rookie runner buffs. These temporary boosts increase the amount of experience and coins you earn, allowing you to level up your favorite Cookies and Pets much faster. Focus on upgrading one or two reliable Cookies early on rather than spreading your resources too thin.

Second, do not underestimate the importance of Treasures. While upgrading Cookies increases your base health and Pet abilities provide utility, Treasures offer the percentage-based score multipliers necessary to compete in the high-tier leagues. Experiment with different combinations to find a setup that complements your run style.

Finally, practice makes perfect. Because the level layouts remain consistent, memorizing the placement of obstacles and giant health potions is the key to surviving the later stages of a run. Keep practicing, learn the patterns, and you will see your scores rise steadily.
