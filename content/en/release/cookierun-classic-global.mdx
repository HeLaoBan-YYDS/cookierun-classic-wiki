## Returning to the Witch's Oven: What is CookieRun Classic?

The release of the **CookieRun Classic global** edition marks a return to the roots of a franchise that has accumulated over 300 million players worldwide. Unlike modern iterations that focus on kingdom-building or tactical team battles, this title brings the focus back to pure platforming. Your primary goal is simple: help your Cookie escape the Witch's Oven, collect Jellies, dodge obstacles, and run as far as you can.

The beauty of this classic edition lies in its accessibility. The control scheme is incredibly straightforward, utilizing only two primary inputs: Jump and Slide. 

*   **Jump:** Tap the jump button to leap over pits, spikes, and obstacles. Double-tap to perform a double jump for higher platforms.
*   **Slide:** Hold the slide button to duck under low-hanging obstacles and slide through narrow passages.

By mastering the timing of these two simple inputs, you can navigate increasingly difficult stages. The game challenges your reflexes as the speed increases and obstacles become more unpredictable.
