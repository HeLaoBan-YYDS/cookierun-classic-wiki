## Master the Mechanics: Jump, Slide, and Score

To succeed in **CookieRun Classic android**, you must master the rhythm of the game. It isn't just about surviving; it's about optimizing your path to hit every "Bonus Time" letter and collect the highest-value Jellies. 

1.  **Double Jumping:** Most Cookies can jump twice in mid-air. This is essential for reaching high-altitude Jellies or clearing large gaps.
2.  **Slide Timing:** Sliding reduces your hitbox, allowing you to pass under low-hanging obstacles. However, you cannot jump while sliding, so your timing must be precise.
3.  **Bonus Time:** Collecting the letters that spell out "BONUS TIME" transports you to a safe area filled with massive amounts of Jellies and Coins. This is where the biggest score jumps happen.
4.  **Health Management:** Your Cookie’s health depletes over time and whenever you hit an obstacle. Collecting health potions is vital for a long-distance run.

| Action | Best Used For... | Pro Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Single Jump** | Small obstacles and low platforms. | Tap lightly for a short hop. |
| **Double Jump** | Reaching "Bonus Time" letters. | Wait for the peak of the first jump. |
| **Slide** | Passing through narrow tunnels. | Don't slide longer than necessary. |
| **Collision** | (Avoid at all costs!) | If you hit an obstacle, you lose speed and HP. |
