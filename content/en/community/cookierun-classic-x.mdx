## Gameplay Mechanics: Jump, Slide, and Score

In the world of **CookieRun Classic x**, mastering the two-button control scheme is essential for climbing the leaderboard. You only need to use two inputs: Jump (on the left) and Slide (on the right). While this sounds simple, the level design quickly increases in difficulty, requiring precise timing to avoid hazards like swinging pendulums, falling platforms, and fiery obstacles.

As you run, your Cookie's energy constantly drains. To keep running, you must collect health potions scattered throughout the stage. Missing a single potion or colliding with an obstacle can prematurely end your run. Therefore, memorizing stage layouts and optimizing your path to collect maximum Jellies is the key to setting high scores.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What makes CookieRun Classic x different from CookieRun: OvenBreak?
Unlike OvenBreak, which features a massive roster of characters, complex guild systems, and various game modes, this classic version focuses on the simple, original gameplay loop. It strips away the bloat to offer pure, two-button running action, making it much easier for casual players to pick up and play.

### How do I unlock the exclusive K-Culture Cookies?
K-Culture Cookies can be unlocked through special launch events, daily login rewards, or by using crystals in the shop. Keep an eye on the event tab, as many of these characters are tied to limited-time challenges that reward rookie runners.

### Is CookieRun Classic x free to play?
Yes, the game is free to download and play on mobile devices. While it contains in-app purchases for crystals, coins, and upgrade packages, players can easily progress and enjoy all content by participating in daily events and climbing the competitive League.

### How do I manage game notifications?
If you want to turn notifications on or off to stay updated on game events without being interrupted, you can manage this in your device settings. Navigate to Settings > Apps > CookieRun > Notifications, and toggle the permission according to your preference.
