## Top Tips and Strategies for Returning Players

If you are stepping back into the running shoes after a multi-year hiatus, the gameplay might feel faster than you remember. The key to mastering the classic run lies in prioritizing your early-game upgrades. Spending your coins wisely in the initial weeks will save you hours of grinding later on.

To maximize your efficiency, we recommend focusing on your basic upgrades before spending all your resources on pulling new cookies. The table below outlines the recommended upgrade priority path based on experienced player feedback.

| Upgrade Type | Priority | Core Benefit | Recommended Level (Early Game) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Energy Limit Upgrade** | Maximum | Increases the maximum run duration | Level 10+ |
| **Jelly Level Upgrade** | High | Increases the base points awarded for all collected jellies | Level 8+ |
| **Bonus Time Upgrade** | Medium | Extends the duration of the Bonus Time flying stage | Level 5+ |
| **Pet Leveling** | Medium | Enhances pet ability cooldowns and item generation | Level 3+ |

*   **Master the Slide:** Sliding reduces your hitbox significantly. Many players jump unnecessarily when a simple slide would keep them safer and closer to the ground-level jellies.
*   **Do Not Ignore Pets:** A common mistake is focusing entirely on leveling up cookies. Your pet provides essential utility, such as creating health potions or magnetic shields.
*   **Memorize Stage Layouts:** Classic stages do not feature randomized layouts. Memorizing where the trickiest obstacles and largest health potions spawn is the key to breaking into the top tier of the leaderboards.
*   **Save Your Crystals:** While it is tempting to spend crystals on cosmetic skins early on, saving them for Epic-tier cookie chests and pet eggs will yield much better long-term results.

Staying connected with other players discussing strategy on **CookieRun Classic fb** forums will keep you updated on the latest meta-shifts, ensuring you always know which cookie-pet combinations are dominating the leaderboards.
