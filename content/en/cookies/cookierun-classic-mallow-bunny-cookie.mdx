## Character Overview and Lore

Originally released to celebrate the Happy 12th Anniversary of the Kakao version of the game (often referred to by the community as CookieRun Classic), the **CookieRun Classic mallow bunny cookie** quickly captured the hearts of players. Known in Korean as the "Mellow Bunny Cookie," she is driven by an insatiable curiosity for exploring uncharted territories. Her character lore highlights her love for discovering mysterious rabbit hole portals, which allow her to travel to whimsical new locations. For her, the memories and landscapes she collects during her journeys are her most prized possessions.

Unlike typical characters who are upgraded using standard in-game gold, this Cookie features a unique progression system. She is only the second Cookie in the history of the classic edition to bypass coin-based upgrades entirely, following in the footsteps of the eleventh-anniversary character, Strawberry Shortcake Cookie.

## Skill Mechanics: Navigating the Bunny Tunnels

The core gameplay loop of the **CookieRun Classic mallow bunny cookie** revolves around her ability to summon rabbit hole portals, which she calls "Bunny Tunnels." These portals appear at fixed intervals during a run, offering both defensive utility and scoring opportunities. 

When running normally, passing through these standard portals provides:
* **Stamina Recovery:** A small boost to keep your run going longer.
* **Coin Generation:** Extra coins to boost your post-run earnings.
* **Marshmallow Jellies:** Special high-value jellies that significantly increase your overall score.

The real magic happens when you encounter a giant rabbit hole portal. Entering this large gateway teleports the Cookie to a "Mysterious World." In this sub-stage, the gameplay mechanics shift dramatically. Your character gains access to multi-stage jumps and a powerful magnetic effect that pulls in all nearby items. The screen fills with coins and marshmallow jellies, allowing you to rapidly accumulate points without the risk of hitting standard obstacles.

## Strategic Gameplay Tips for High Scores

To get the most out of your runs with the **CookieRun Classic mallow bunny cookie**, keep these strategic tips in mind:

1. **Master the Multi-Stage Jumps:** Once you enter the Mysterious World, your jump mechanics change. Practice timing your multi-stage jumps to sweep the top and bottom lanes of the screen, ensuring you do not leave any marshmallow jellies behind.
2. **Leverage the Magnetic Effect:** Because the Mysterious World activates a magnetic aura, you do not need to stress about precisely hitting every single coin. Instead, focus on navigating safely and letting the magnet do the work.
3. **Optimize Your Treasures:** Equip treasures that complement jelly scores or extend your life. Since the Combi Bonus already slows down energy drain, stacking additional stamina-preservation treasures can result in exceptionally long runs.
4. **Prioritize Bunny Egg Pieces:** Because you cannot use gold coins to upgrade this Cookie, keep an eye out for events and reward packages that yield Bunny Egg Pieces. Maxing out her level should be your top priority to increase her base energy to 144.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I upgrade the CookieRun Classic mallow bunny cookie?
Unlike most characters in the game who require Coins for upgrades, the **CookieRun Classic mallow bunny cookie** requires a unique resource called Bunny Egg Pieces. You will need 300 Bunny Egg Pieces to upgrade her from Level 1 to Level 2.

### What does the Mysterious World skill do?
When the Cookie enters a giant rabbit hole portal, she is transported to the Mysterious World. In this special zone, she gains access to multi-stage jumping capabilities and a magnetic field that automatically attracts nearby coins and jellies, allowing for massive point accumulation.

### What is the best pet to pair with this Cookie?
The best pet pairing is the S-grade **Soft Egg Bunny**. Combining these two triggers a Combi Bonus that grants extra points for all collected Jellies and reduces your overall energy depletion rate by 8%.
