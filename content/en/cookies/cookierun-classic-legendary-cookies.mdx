### Key Changes to Star Upgrading

| Feature | Old System | New System (v7.4+) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Soulstone Conversion** | N/A | 1:1.2 Rate |

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the best ways to get CookieRun Classic legendary cookies?
The most reliable methods include participating in limited-time Event Gachas, where rates are boosted, and consistently checking the Rainbow Shell Gallery and Guild Medal Shop for Soulstones.

Yes, under the current v7.4+ system, legendary units receive new appearances when reaching 2, 4, and 6 stars. Additionally, their backgrounds change once you promote them to 3 stars.

Most are available, but keep in mind that newly released units are typically excluded from the standard gacha until their specific event banner concludes.

Absolutely. With the new 6-star promotion system, even older legendary units have been brought up to modern standards, making them highly competitive in both PvE and PvP modes.
