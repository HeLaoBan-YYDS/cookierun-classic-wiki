## Understanding CookieRun Classic Gameplay Mechanics

Before diving into the characters, it is essential to understand how the gameplay loop operates. Unlike the more complex RPG mechanics of spin-offs like CookieRun: Kingdom, this game is a pure, side-scrolling endless runner. Your character runs automatically from left to right. Your only inputs are two buttons: Jump and Slide. 

Your cookie has a health bar that constantly depletes over time. Colliding with obstacles like spiked barriers or falling into bottomless pits will drastically reduce your health. To keep running, you must collect health potions scattered throughout the stages while gathering as many jellies and coins as possible. 

| Mechanic | Description | Primary Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Jump Button** | Makes your cookie leap over low obstacles and reach high jellies. | Obstacle avoidance & vertical movement |
| **Slide Button** | Makes your cookie slide under low ceilings and high obstacles. | Underpass navigation & dodging |
| **Energy Bar** | A constantly draining life bar that acts as a timer for your run. | Determines maximum run duration |
| **Jellies & Coins** | Items scattered across the map that provide points and currency. | High score generation & upgrades |
| **Health Potions** | Power-ups that restore a portion of your energy bar. | Extending run times |

When looking at the roster, the most effective CookieRun Classic beginner cookies prioritize survival and high jelly collection rates. Because early-game resources are scarce, investing in characters that offer safety nets or passive coin generation is the smartest way to progress.
